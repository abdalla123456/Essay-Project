List of HTML tags: self closing and "containing"
see W3 Schools (https://www.w3schools.com/html/default.asp)
Notice formatting is included here
- br, !
- h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, & h6
- p, a, b or strong, i or em
- ul, ol; both with li
- More Specific Attributes for Anchors (<a></a>), img, and URL or Pathways
  href (URL, Pathway, target, "_blank"
  img, scr="URL", alt="URL" , Pathway"
  Note: try using picutre instead of img
  See: https://www.w3schools.com/tags/tag_picture.asp
- More advanced Anchor Elements
  For language: hreflang
  For downloading: download
- Extras Tags, in addition to p
  subscript: sub
  superscript: sup
- Extra attributes for Lists
  <ol type="1|a|A|i|I"> <!Different numbering styles>
  <ul style="list-style-type:disc|circle|square|none"></ul> <!Intro to CSS>
